Patch Management

Ensure all devices are running the latest security patches. Relion’s patch management module automatically scans your network to identify devices and their patch status. Scheduled or urgent patches can be deployed remotely to keep your environment protected.

Procurement

Streamline your procurement process from request to receipt. The procurement module allows department heads to submit purchase requests that are routed for approval based on defined workflows. Asset details are automatically added to your inventory upon receipt.

HAM

Keep detailed hardware records like purchase date, warranty and contract details, department and user assignments, and location. Generate barcode labels for physical tagging and track assets throughout their lifecycle from purchase to disposal.

SAM

Keep detailed hardware records like purchase date, warranty and contract details, department and user assignments, and location. Generate barcode labels for physical tagging and track assets throughout their lifecycle from purchase to disposal.

Network Management

Monitor network devices, bandwidth usage, link utilization and more from a single dashboard. Configure alerts for link failures or performance issues. Access control lists prevent unauthorized access.

Data Theft Detection

Remotely manage and secure mobile devices on your network including cell phones and tablets. Enroll devices, deploy apps, retire assets, and wipe devices if lost or stolen -all from your Relion dashboard.

Compliance

Scheduled or on-demand scans check for compliance with software audits, hardware warranties, security patches and other IT policies. Detailed reports provide audit-ready documentation and proof of due diligence.

Remote Management

Administer remote devices from anywhere without traveling on-site. Remotely monitor, troubleshoot, update and even power cycle devices using Relion’s remote management module.

Reports

Generate over 50 predefined reports on asset inventory, software compliance, network health, procurement, and more. Reports can be scheduled, customized and exported in multiple formats for record-keeping and audits.

Helpdesk System

Streamline support requests with Relion’s integrated helpdesk module. Ticket submissions are linked to specific assets for faster resolution. Customizable workflows route tickets to the appropriate teams or individuals.
